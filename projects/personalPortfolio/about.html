Mind Flayer

Many times I tend to find my myself digging into the mysteries of the universe and debating the nature of human existence. I like to engage people on the taboo subjects of religion and politics. I prefer to keep my convictions loosely held so I can easily adapt to new information and stay engaged in the continuous circle of life. I really like conspiracy theories for both an intellectual basis as well as the opportunity to see how deeply the rabbit hole goes.

Dungeon Master

Gaming is a passion of mine. I particularly like MMORPGs although they can suck your life away. I have played them since the old days of MUDs. My first large scale MMO was Ultima Online which was quickly followed by Everquest, Asheron's Call, and many others to this day.

Journeyman Coder

I love development, though I tend to get a little scatter-brained when it comes to sticking with one technology. I am currently studying web development as well as Unity 3D development. Additionally, I have programmed in C, C++, C#, PHP, Ruby, Javascript, Lua, Java, Objective C, and a long time ago, ActionScript.

Current Work

Future Work

Hopefully I will move into full-time development in the next year. Past that, I want to continue working on my company and develop my own game using Unity 3D.
